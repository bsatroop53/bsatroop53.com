130 Scouts and Scouters had a great time at the Yankee Doodle and Fort Orange District "I Love My Park Camporee" at Lake Taghkanic State Park. The Scouts helped out with service projects and had great Scouting activities. The scouts also met the State Park Commissioner, Kathy Mosher.

### Photos

The photos below came from the Yankee Doodle District's [Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/yankeebsa/posts/pfbid0dk8bRuxFsF5dXfsxYiE9YP4YFaVdhhnrLcZ6wb81nZFSSpCifzUfNJGBS82kMCXCl).  They contain scouts from both Troop 53 and some other troops as well.
