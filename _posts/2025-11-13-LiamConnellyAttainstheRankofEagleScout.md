## Town of Schodack Resolution 2025-332 

WHEREAS, Liam Connelly, son of Mark and Meghan Connelly on the 13th of
November in the year two thousand twenty-five, was awarded the rank of Eagle
Scout, the highest rank a boy can achieve in scouting; and

WHEREAS, Liam started his journey in scouting as a Webelo in 2018, with
Pack 253, and

WHEREAS, Liam has earned the Arrow of Light award the highest Cub Scout
honor, and

WHEREAS, Liam is a member of Boy Scout Troop 53, and has earned 30 Merit
Badges in his 7 years of Boy Scouting, and

WHEREAS, Liam has served the troop as Senior Patrol Leader, Patrol Leader,
and Instructor, and

WHEREAS, as his Eagle Scout project, Liam, with the assistance of other
Scouts, Scout leaders, and family and friends, built two eight-foot-long benches
for the pickleball courts at the Schodack Town Park, and

WHEREAS Liam is a senior at Maple Hill High School, where he is a member
of the track team, President of the National Honor Society, and will be attending
SUNY Geneseo next fall, and

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Town Board of the Town of
Schodack commends and congratulates Liam Connelly upon the auspicious
occasion of being awarded the rank of Eagle Scout, Boy Scouts of America;
and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that Supervisor Peter will transmit a copy of this
resolution, suitably engrossed to Liam Connelly.

* *Resolution was made during a Schodack Town Board meeting on December 30, 2025.*
